Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

WatchLater ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address (for account identification)
  • Account ID (for authentication)

Notion Integration

When you connect Notion, we receive:

  • OAuth access token (to save summaries to your Notion workspace)
  • Database ID (to know where to save summaries)

We do not access any other data in your Notion workspace.

Usage Data

We track:

  • Number of summaries created (for quota management)
  • YouTube URLs processed (stored temporarily for processing only)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the summarization service
  • To save summaries to your Notion workspace
  • To manage your subscription and usage limits
  • To improve our service

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) - hosted in the US
  • Railway (API hosting) - encrypted in transit and at rest

Authentication tokens are stored locally in your device's secure Keychain.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In - for authentication
  • Notion API - to save summaries to your workspace
  • YouTube - to fetch video transcripts (publicly available data)
  • Google Gemini AI - to generate summaries (transcripts are processed but not stored by Google)

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your Notion integration at any time

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
